About the Ministry of Tourism

The Ministry of Tourism is dedicated to promoting Listenbourg as a premier travel destination and supporting the development of the country's tourism industry. We work to showcase the beauty and diversity of Listenbourg and provide resources and support for travelers and industry partners.

Listenbourg is a coastal country located near Spain and Portugal, with a population of 79 million people and a total area of 642940 km^2. French is the main language spoken in the country, and the Euro is the currency used.

Listenbourg has a rich and diverse culture, with beautiful landscapes, vibrant cities, and a long history. From the mountains of Kusterde to the beaches of Adrias, there is something for everyone in Listenbourg. We invite you to come and explore all that our country has to offer.

Popular Destinations in Listenbourg

Lurenberg

The capital city of Listenbourg, Lurenberg is a vibrant and cosmopolitan destination with a rich history and culture. From the museums and galleries of Old Town to the shopping and dining of the modern city center, there is something for everyone in Lurenberg.

Mitteland Mountains

Located in the province of Mitteland, the Mitteland Mountains offer a breathtaking landscape of rugged peaks and picturesque valleys. From hiking and mountain climbing to skiing and snowboarding, there's something for everyone in the Mitteland Mountains.

Flußerde Beaches

The province of Flußerde is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in Listenbourg. From the bustling resorts of the north to the secluded coves of the south, the beaches of Flußerde offer something for every type of traveler. Don't forget to try the local seafood while you're here!
